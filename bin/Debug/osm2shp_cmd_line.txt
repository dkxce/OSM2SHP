Open config and convert:

  OSM2SHP.exe /convert
  OSM2SHP.exe /convert <file.o2s> 
  OSM2SHP.exe /convert <file.pbf> <file.dbf> 
  OSM2SHP.exe /convert <file.osm> <file.dbf>
  OSM2SHP.exe /convert <file.o2s> <file.pbf> <file.dbf> 
  OSM2SHP.exe /convert <file.o2s> <file.osm> <file.dbf>

Open config, convert & close:

  OSM2SHP.exe /convert
  OSM2SHP.exe /convert /close <file.o2s> 
  OSM2SHP.exe /convert /close <file.pbf> <file.dbf> 
  OSM2SHP.exe /convert /close <file.osm> <file.dbf>
  OSM2SHP.exe /convert /close <file.o2s> <file.pbf> <file.dbf> 
  OSM2SHP.exe /convert /close <file.o2s> <file.osm> <file.dbf>
